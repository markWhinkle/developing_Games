Dead by Daylight Review

Travis, Apr 30, 2020

Dead by Daylight is a strategic horror game that you have to work as a team and use your skills to help you survive or hunt. You can play as the survivors or you can play as the killers. When you play as survivors, your goal is to try and escape by doing all five of the generators and open the exit gates and leave. When you play killer, your goal is to interrupt those survivors from completing the generators and sacrifice those survivors to the entity. There are a wide selection of killers and survivors, some even from famous horror movies like the Halloween movie and Nightmare on Elm Street movie. The best way I can describe this game in how I look at it is that it’s bad sometimes but it’s also really good other times, it’s also really damn addictive. Both sides have a lot of power when they want to, which can make the game be over in like a few minutes which is not fun at all. Besides that, this game is really fun, I got it like probably almost a year ago and I have about 1292 hours in it, so that shows how addictive the game can be. Give the game a try, it’s not very expensive at the moment and you never know, you might actually enjoy it like how I do.
